  • What is GobaPay?

    Gobapay is a platform initiated for business owners who affiliate with Glittarz to be able to monitor payments and know offers Glittarz have to offer their business at that particular time

  • How do I sign up for GobaPay?

    Signing up for GobaPay is easy! Simply visit www.gobapay.com and follow the registration prompts to create your account. You’ll need a valid email address and phone number to get started.

  • Is GobaPay secure?

    Yes, GobaPay is highly secure. We use advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information and transactions. Your data is safe with us, and we continuously monitor our systems to prevent unauthorized access.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, online transfers, and mobile payment platforms for your convenience.

  • Are there any fees for using GobaPay?

    Yes, transactions with GobaPay attracts a little fee which enables the company run the online and offline promotion for each business registered with us. Please refer to our fee schedule on the app or website to get full details

  • Does GobaPay offer loan schemes?

    No, GOBA Pay is not currently issuing out loans. But all Glittarz customers will be notified when GOBA starts issuing out Loans.
